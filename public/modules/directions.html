Instructions

Please set your browser to full screen and stay on the page until the end of the study.

Speed Test

The goal of the Speed Test is to observe your performance on a word selection task at different speeds. The word selection task consists of a scrolling list of words moving at a certain speed. Your task will be to select as many fruits as possible in the list of words. You can select and deselect words before they go off-screen by clicking and unclicking them. After completing the current level, you will either move on to more advanced levels or be redirected to the Reasoning Study. Note that speed increases with levels.

Reasoning Study

This part of the study consists of a series of 9 tasks consisting of a simultanous task and a questionnaire.

Simultanous Tasks

In this part of the study, you will be shown a prompt and some data. Once you have read the prompt and looked at the data, click on "I'm ready to see the question" to begin the response section, which consists of two simultaneous tasks . You will be asked to answer two questions about the data and to complete the word selection task at the same time.

Remember that the questions are worth more points than the word selection task. While you should attempt both, you should priotize answering the questions correctly. Once you are confident with your answers to the two questions, hit "Submit".

Questionnaire

After you hit "Submit", you will be asked a few questions about the Simultaneous Tasks. Please answer them as truthfully as possible.

Spatial Ability Test

This test consists of a series of questions to test your capacity to understand, reason, and remember the spatial relations among objects or space.
